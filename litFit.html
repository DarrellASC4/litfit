About

What we do

We are four coders that came together to create a groundbreaking application: Lit Fit.

Lit Fit was inspired by a controversial sweater that a member of our group wore to All Star Code one day. He wore a turtleneck on a blazing hot summer day and he insisted that his outfit was lit, even when everyone told him it wasn't. We thought that it would be better if we had more opinions to settle the matter, thats how Lit Fit was brought to life.

Through Lit Fit we are helping millions easily plan outfits for the future and inspire new styles.
